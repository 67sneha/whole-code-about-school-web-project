    Hello parents, admission to Golden International School means a great opportunity for your child to truly grow. Here’s how to go about it, along with information about fee structure and schedule.

    PROCEDURE

    Admission Process (GIS CAT Road)
    1. Make an enquiry, call us at 9669196698, 9669296698, or mail us at contact@gisindore.in
    2. Schedule a school tour
    3. Submit your Application Form
    4. Entrance Exam (For Classes 9 th to 12 th )
    5. Class 10 th and 12 th is a Direct Admission process in CBSE (Only in case of Transfer)
    6. Confirm your admission by submitting admission form along with the required documents.
    Admission Process (GIS Pre-Primary Navlakha)
    1. Make an enquiry
    2. Schedule a school tour
    3. Submit your Application Form
    4. Confirm your admission by submitting admission form along with the required documents.
